About FlyZen

Flyzen was founded with a mission to empower passengers and improve the way airlines respond to flight disruptions. We offer a simple and effective way to deal with flight delays and follow 3 core principles: Information. Benefits. Recovery.

We see an alignment of interests between passengers and airlines in dealing with flight disruption and this is our core philosophy. We also collaborate with airlines and offer their passengers a successful and effective solution in how to avoid and deal with flight frustration.

It is our passion to make a difference in the travel industry, and we are driven to constantly evolve and improve our service and products.
